Welcome to Twi Level 1

About Twi

Twi is a dialect of the Akan language spoken in southern Ghana. It is one of the most widely spoken languages in Ghana with over 9 million speakers.

Cultural Importance

Twi is not just a language; it's a gateway to Ghana's rich Akan culture. It connects you to traditional proverbs, folklore, and customs that have been passed down through generations.

Why Learn Twi?

Twi is widely spoken throughout southern Ghana, making it essential for communication in cities like Kumasi and Accra. Learning Twi opens doors to understanding Ghanaian culture and connecting with millions of people.
